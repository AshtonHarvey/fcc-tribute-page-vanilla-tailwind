The history of the mouse

  • The trackball, a related pointing device, was invented in 1946 by Ralph Benjamin
  • The device was patented in 1947
  • Douglas Engelbart of the Stanford Research Institute has been credited as the inventor of the computer mouse.
  • The Xerox Alto was one of the first computers designed for individual use in 1973 and is regarded as the first modern computer to use a mouse.
